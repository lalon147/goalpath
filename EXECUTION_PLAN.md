# GoalPath 12-Week Delivery Sprint Plan

## Executive Summary
- **Project:** GoalPath (Personal Goal & Habit Tracking Mobile Application)
- **Timeline:** 12 weeks 
- **Tech Stack:** React Native (mobile) + Node.js/Express (backend) + MongoDB (database)
- **Target:** Adults 18+
- **Deployment:** TestFlight (iOS) + Google Play Internal Testing
- **Total Hours:** 120 hours (spread across 12 weeks at ~10 hours/week)

---

- ✅ More comprehensive testing
- ✅ Reduced team burnout risk
- ✅ Better documentation
- ✅ Time for iterations and refinements

**The project is more likely to succeed with the 12-week timeline.**
